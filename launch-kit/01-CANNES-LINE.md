# The Cannes Line

## The 12-Word Sentence That Stops a Room

---

> **"We taught AI to think like a creative director — not replace one."**

---

## Alternate Versions

### For Innovation Juries
> "The first AI that understands why great work works."

### For CCO Presentations
> "What if your creative intuition had a research department that never sleeps?"

### For New Business Theater
> "Every campaign decision, backed by every Cannes Lion ever won."

### For the Press Release
> "TBWA builds AI that thinks in effectiveness, not efficiency."

### For the Elevator (5 seconds)
> "It's like having the entire Cannes archive whisper strategy in your ear."

---

## How to Deliver It

**Pause before.**
Say it slower than feels natural.
Let the silence after do the work.

The line isn't meant to explain.
It's meant to make them lean forward and say:
*"Wait — show me."*

---

## The Follow-Up Line (Optional)

After the pause:

> "Let me show you what that looks like in 60 seconds."

Then launch the demo.
